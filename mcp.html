Elevated Dementia Risk and Cognitive Decline in Multisite Chronic Pain

Wenhui Zhao, Lei Zhao, Xiangyu Chang, Xuejing Lu, Yiheng Tu

Summary

Nearly half of chronic pain patients report pain at multiple anatomical locations, referred to as multisite chronic pain (MCP)...

Key Statistics

Dementia Risk

Individuals with MCP had a 1.36-fold higher risk of dementia compared to pain-free controls.

Cognitive Decline

Accelerated cognitive decline was observed in individuals with MCP compared to those with single-site chronic pain (SCP) and pain-free individuals.

Hippocampal Atrophy

Significant atrophy in the hippocampus was noted in MCP patients, correlating with increased dementia risk.
